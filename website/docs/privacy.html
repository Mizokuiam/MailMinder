MailMinder Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

Introduction

MailMinder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect and process the following types of information:

Authentication Information

  • What: Google account authentication credentials
  • Purpose: To authenticate users and provide access to Gmail services
  • Storage: Credentials are handled securely through Google's OAuth system
  • Retention: Only during active sessions

Personal Communications

  • What: Email content and metadata
  • Purpose: For AI-powered categorization and organization
  • Processing: All processing is done locally on your device
  • Storage: No email content is stored on our servers

User Activity

  • What:
    • Email management actions
    • Feature usage patterns
    • Interface interactions
  • Purpose: To improve user experience and extension functionality
  • Storage: Anonymized and stored locally

Website Content

  • What: Gmail interface elements
  • Purpose: To integrate our features with Gmail
  • Processing: Local processing only
  • Storage: Temporary, in-browser only

Data Protection

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No personal data is sold or transferred to third parties
  • Data is not used for any purposes unrelated to email management
  • No data is used for creditworthiness assessment or lending purposes

Data Storage and Security

  • User preferences and settings are stored locally
  • Industry-standard encryption for any data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No cloud storage of personal data

User Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Control feature permissions

Third-Party Services

We only interact with:

  • Gmail API (for email management)
  • Google OAuth (for authentication)

Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the extension interface.
